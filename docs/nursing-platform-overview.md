# DoctaRx Nursing Education & Clinical Training Platform

DoctaRx Nursing Education & Clinical Training Platform is a Nigeria-focused academic and clinical training module for nursing institutions. It combines student profiles, a moderated academic timeline, nursing LMS content, assignments, gradebook workflows, course discussions, virtual clinical simulation, telehealth skills practice, digital clinical logbook workflows, lecturer review tools, HOD reporting, certificates, and payment/access tracking.

## Pilot Use Case

The first seeded tenant is University of Abuja, Department of Nursing Science. The module is structured so additional universities, colleges of nursing, teaching hospital nursing schools, midwifery schools, and post-basic nursing programmes can be added later.

## Users

- Super Admin
- Institution Admin
- HOD / Department Admin
- Lecturer
- Clinical Coordinator
- Clinical Supervisor / Preceptor
- Student Nurse
- DoctaRx Support Admin

## Main Modules

- Institution, department, academic session, and cohort management
- Full student profile workspace with bio, skills, interests, notifications, academic records, and activity
- Facebook-style academic timeline with scoped posts, comments, reactions, attachments, reporting, and moderation
- Nursing LMS with courses, course sections, lessons, resources, enrollments, and progress
- Assignments, submissions, lecturer grading, grade comments, and student gradebook
- Course discussion board and Q&A threads
- Quizzes, materials, and analytics
- Nigerian virtual clinical simulation cases using fictional sample patients
- Mock telehealth consultation room with triage and communication checklists
- Digital clinical logbook with supervisor review and skill sign-off
- Certificate of Participation and Certificate of Completion records
- HOD reports and 90-day review readiness dashboard
- Payment/access tracking for pilot operations

## Pricing Assumptions

- NGN 15,000 all-access pilot student fee
- NGN 30,000 annual student access
- NGN 15,000,000 departmental license
- NGN 35,000 certificate course fee

These are planning assumptions for pilot and institutional discussions. Public UI avoids private company revenue projections.
