# Nursing Platform Roadmap

## Phase 1

University of Abuja MVP pilot with seeded institution, nursing LMS, simulation cases, telehealth skills lab, digital logbook, HOD dashboard, certificates, demo users, and payment/access tracking.

## Phase 2

Affiliated nursing-school rollout with additional departments, clinical placement partners, supervisor onboarding, cohort import tooling, and stronger report exports.

## Phase 3

National nursing education platform for federal nursing schools, colleges of nursing, midwifery schools, private universities, teaching hospital nursing schools, and post-basic nursing programmes.

## Phase 4

Advanced telehealth simulation, CPD programmes, research analytics, external accreditation evidence packs, richer certificate verification, and production-grade institutional billing.
