# Nursing Platform Student Guide

## Access

- Open `/ng/nursing/login`.
- Use a seeded student account from `docs/nursing-platform-demo-credentials.md`.
- Student routes resolve to `/ng/nursing/student`.

## Core Workflow

1. Update the Profile tab with phone, bio, interests, and skills.
2. Use Timeline for cohort questions, professional posts, comments, and helpful reactions.
3. Use LMS to open courses, review sections, complete lessons, and track progress.
4. Use Assignments to submit written work.
5. Use Gradebook to review marks and lecturer feedback.
6. Use Q&A for course-specific discussion threads.
7. Use Quizzes, Simulation, Telehealth Lab, and Logbook for clinical learning activities.
8. Use Certificates and Payments to review eligibility and access status.

## Student Permissions

Students can complete lessons, submit assignments, post to the academic timeline, reply to discussions, take quizzes, complete simulations, submit logbook entries, and view their own profile, gradebook, certificates, and payment status.

Students cannot grade submissions, moderate timeline posts, manage courses, approve logbook entries, or view institution-wide reporting.
